## FR1: Emergency Reporting
Citizens must be able to report emergencies such as injuries, trapped people,
or infrastructure damage using mobile or web interfaces.

## FR2: Real-Time Alert Distribution
The system must send alerts and notifications to rescue teams and authorities
based on location and severity.

## FR3: Rescue Team Coordination
Emergency coordinators must be able to assign tasks to rescue teams and track
their status in real time.

## FR4: Shelter and Hospital Management
The system must maintain updated information about available shelters and
hospital capacities.

## FR5: Situation Dashboard
Authorities must have access to a dashboard showing disaster status, reports,
resource availability, and response progress.

## FR6: External System Integration
The system must integrate with external services such as weather APIs and
telecommunication providers.
